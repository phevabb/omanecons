K.K.A LOGISTICS

KKA Logistics is the engineering solutions, engineering accessories and supply chain arm of the company. Our services include:

  • Hot dip galvanization of steel materials
  • Tower painting and maintenance
  • General fabrication utilities and formworks
  • Construction of tower foundations for installation of communications and power transmission towers
  • Supply of towers/accessories and chemical anchors
  • End-to-end project management

KKA Logistics has the capacity and capability to design and manufacture any customized component or accessory that may be required to complete any engineering work in the industry we operate in.

KKA Logistics is also the supply chain arm of the company. Providing timely logistics and haulage services to our customers. With our huge fleets of trucks and experience, we are that partner you can trust.
