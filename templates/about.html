OUR HISTORY & FACTS

K.K.A Group was established in early 2014 with a vision to deliver comprehensive engineering and technical services across Ghana. As the parent company, K.K.A Group oversees three specialized branches: K.K.A Engineering, K.K.A Logistics, and K.K.A Handyman. From its inception, K.K.A has been committed to offering total engineering solutions to government, commercial, and residential clients. Our services range from the supply of engineering accessories and civil/structural engineering to hands-on technical support like plumbing and generator servicing. Backed by a skilled team of engineers and responsive operations staff, we continue to deliver reliable, professional, and timely solutions across all sectors. .
