About KKA Group

K.K.A Group was established early 2014. K.K.A Group as a mother company strives to provide Government, Commercial and Residential property owners a total structural and civil engineering solutions, from Road Construction to fixing technical problems from Plumbing to Generator servicing with a prompt customer service response. With over 10 years’ experience in the construction industry with very keen customers, we are your one-stop shop for all your construction and engineering needs. We are your partner to trust with all your complex construction and logistical needs. We are certified with Ghana’s EPA, trained Job Safety officers both on sites and offices. We also conduct regular training sessions in-house and customers who updates their HSE/JSA and MS on regular basis. We have the best technology systems in place that eases stress of servicing customers which includes mobile apps for requests till completion of work. We also use modern material technologies to provide our services. We are your partner to trust with all your constructional needs.
